GOOD Maker is a tool to help you make good things happen.

  1. Organizations create challenges for the community, asking for whatever is needed to drive meaningful impact.

  2. The community submits ideas, plans, and designs to meet a given challenge.

  3. The community then votes on the idea they believe meets the challenge best.

  4. The winner is provided an award to activate their idea into real-world impact.
